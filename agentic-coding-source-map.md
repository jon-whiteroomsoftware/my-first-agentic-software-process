# Agentic Coding Guide: Source Map

The guide draws from 99 unique sent messages to Dave Sparks, Ted Barnett, and Jonathan Tien dated January 1 through July 21, 2026. All messages were screened by subject and snippet. The 51 most relevant candidates were read in full; personal chatter, duplicate forwards, short acknowledgements, and unrelated material were excluded from the curriculum.

This map lists the source emails that contributed the most language or structure. Dates reflect the sender's mailbox timestamps. Links open the original message in Gmail for the connected account.

## Material intentionally kept out of the main path

The emails contain useful but rapidly aging details about exact model rankings, plan prices, subscription resets, product rebranding, cloud command syntax, and feature availability. The guide converts most of these into durable principles and keeps a short dated setup snapshot. Exact July 2026 claims remain available in the source messages above.

Several emails also contain quoted replies from recipients. Those replies were used only to understand what a passage referred to; their personal material and prose were not incorporated into the guide.
